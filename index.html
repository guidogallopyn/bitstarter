Game Developers: add VocalSpellzz to your game

The VocalSpellzz web-service makes it easy to empower your game with VocalSpellz technology. Audio with the spoken spell cast is streamed to the cloud where the intended spell is recognized and the spoken spell is returned to your game with an indication of strength. In addition there is a wizard apprentice service that allows you to teach your gamers how to cast vocal spells. And of course, the VocalSpellzz service allows complete cusomizartions to the magic spells used in your game.
