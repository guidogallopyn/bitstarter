Gamers: cast powerfull spoken magic spells in your favorite online game

In a VocalSpellzz empowerd game, spellcasting characters first go to Magic School to learn the ancient art of spell casting by using the power of you voice. In the heat of the battle, Vocalspellzz gives you the power to cast powerfull magic spells by voice by using the microphone you use for online gaming. With your liking and backing, more games will adopt VocalSpellzz, and as a reward you will receive an exclusive magic spell, only available to VocalSpellzz backers, when your game of choice enables VocalSpellzz.

Game Developers: improve gameplay and gain an innovative edge

The VocalSpellzz web-service makes it easy to empower your game with VocalSpellz technology. Audio with the spoken spell cast is streamed to the VocalSpellzz cloud where the intended spell is recognized and returned to your game with an indication of strength.In addition VocalSpellz has a wizard apprentice service that teaches gamers how to cast spoken spells. Of course, the VocalSpellzz service allows complete customization to the magic spells used in your game and the language used by magic characters in your game.
